Textbook Alternative Exon Finder

View project onGitHub

Aim

TAEF is composed by three Perl scripts as a pipeline to drag out the textbook alternative exon.

Textbook alternative exon

TAE means:

  • present in isoform_A while absent from isoform_B
  • flanking two exons in isoform_A just happens to be exactly the same as isoform_B

Note

This pipeline? Just for fun. BTW, intersectBED of BEDTools package should be installed. Perl works like a scaffold.
